4.1.4.2.1.2. Anticholinergic drugs versus conservative treatment. More than 100 RCTs and high-quality reviews are available detailing relative efficacy of anticholinergic drugs and conservative treatment strategies [143,165,171-174]. Most of these were independent studies. The main focus of the reviews was to compare the different drugs used to treat UUI. A U.S. health technology assessment [172] found that the vast majority of trials were of a low or moderate quality.
