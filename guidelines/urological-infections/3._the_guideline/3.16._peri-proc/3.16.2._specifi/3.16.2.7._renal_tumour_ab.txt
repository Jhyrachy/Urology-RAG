3.16.2.7. Renal tumour ablation. One systematic review publication date 2018 included 6,952 patients across 51 studies [552]. Infectious complications were reported in 74 patients including fever (60.8%), abscess (21.6%) and UTI (8.1%). Prophylactic antibiotic use was reported in 5.4% of patients but it was not possible to study its association to infectious complications due to lack of reporting.
