6.3. Summary of evidence for prognosis. Summary of evidenceLEPrognostic factors for survival in primary urethral carcinoma are: age, gender, race, tumour stage and grade, nodal stage, presence of distant metastasis, histological type, tumour size, tumour location, concomitant bladder cancer and type and modality of treatment.3In locally-advanced urothelial- and SCC of the urethra, treatment in academic centres improves OS.3
