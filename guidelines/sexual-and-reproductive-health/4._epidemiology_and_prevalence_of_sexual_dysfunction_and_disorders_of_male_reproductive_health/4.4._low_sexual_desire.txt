4.4. Low sexual desire. The global prevalence of low sexual desire in men is 3-28% [205,233,234]. Low solitary and dyadic sexual desires have been reported in 68% and 14% of men, respectively [235]. Also, low sexual desire has been observed as a common complaint in gay men, with a prevalence of 19-57% [236,237]. Despite its relationship with age, low sexual desire has also been reported among young men (18-29 years), with a prevalence of 6-19% [197,238,239].
