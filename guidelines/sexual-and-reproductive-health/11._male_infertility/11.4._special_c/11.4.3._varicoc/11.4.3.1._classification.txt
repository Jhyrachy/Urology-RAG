11.4.3.1. Classification. The following classification of varicocele [1522] is useful in clinical practice: Subclinical: not palpable or visible at rest or during Valsalva manoeuvre, but can be shown by special tests (Doppler US).Grade 1: palpable during Valsalva manoeuvre.Grade 2: palpable at rest.Grade 3: visible and palpable at rest.
