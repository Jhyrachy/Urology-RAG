8.2. Peri-operative care after treatment of strictures in trans men. Anecdotally, after endoscopic incision and urethroplasty, the urethral catheter is maintained for two to three weeks [496,499]. Peri-catheter urethrography is advised before catheter removal as it might be challenging to reinsert the urethral catheter in case of urinary extravasation [499].
