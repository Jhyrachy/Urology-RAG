4.2. Cancer stage grouping. In the UICC TNM 8th edition, stage II was newly subdivided into Stage IIA and Stage IIB based on T categories. Stage IIA is defined as T1b–2N0M0, while Stage IIB is defined as T3N0M0 [73] (Table 4.2).
